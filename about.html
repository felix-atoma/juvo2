About JUVO

Empowering rural communities in Ghana and beyond with instant health guidance through feature phones—no internet required.

Accessible Health Checks

JUVO provides instant pre-diagnosis support through USSD—accessible on any mobile phone, even without data or smartphones.

Local Language Support

Available in Twi, Fante, and Dagbani to ensure everyone, regardless of literacy or language barriers, can understand and use JUVO.

Reliable Guidance

JUVO offers trusted symptom screening based on structured medical logic, connecting users to the next steps for real care.

Bringing healthcare to your fingertips

JUVO bridges the healthcare gap for rural and underserved populations, providing quick answers when it matters most.

No smartphone, no problem. JUVO runs on basic mobile phones using USSD codes and helps users understand symptoms before deciding to seek care.
