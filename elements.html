Empowering Rural Health

Many people in rural Ghana and surrounding regions struggle to access basic health services—not because they don’t care, but because tools and infrastructure are lacking. That’s where JUVO steps in. Using simple USSD technology, we bridge the gap by delivering trusted health guidance in local languages to any phone—no internet needed.

By providing early symptom checks and nearby care recommendations, JUVO helps prevent minor issues from becoming serious illnesses. It’s fast, free, and built for the realities of underserved communities.

Key Definitions

What is JUVO?

JUVO is a USSD-based mobile health assistant designed for underserved communities. It allows users to access basic pre-diagnosis support and health guidance in local languages—without needing internet access or a smartphone.

What is Pre-Diagnosis?

Pre-diagnosis refers to the early evaluation of symptoms before a formal medical consultation. JUVO helps users understand possible causes of their symptoms and suggests next steps or when to seek urgent care.

What is USSD?

USSD (Unstructured Supplementary Service Data) is a technology that lets people interact with services through simple phone codes like *123#. JUVO uses USSD so anyone with a basic phone can access essential health support—anytime, anywhere.
