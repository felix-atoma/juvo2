How JUVO Is Transforming Healthcare Access in Rural Ghana

  • Health, Mobile Innovation
  • 12 Comments

JUVO is bridging the gap between rural communities and healthcare services using USSD technology, ensuring that even the most underserved populations can get quick, accurate, and culturally relevant health guidance.

In regions where internet access is limited, JUVO provides a reliable alternative. Our USSD-based platform works on any mobile phone, enabling users to check symptoms and receive early health advice in local languages like Twi, Fante, and Dagbani.

“Access to health information shouldn’t depend on internet or smartphone availability. JUVO is here to make sure no one is left behind.”

The JUVO system has already reached hundreds of individuals across Ghana’s rural zones. By offering a simple, menu-driven experience and voice assistance, it caters to both literate and non-literate users.

Our long-term vision includes partnerships with local health authorities and integrating AI to better personalize health insights. The future of rural healthcare starts with solutions that speak directly to the people — and JUVO is leading that change.
